Our Story

Grown Close to Home

Truthware started with a simple frustration: most of what we wear travels further than we ever will, passing through hands and processes nobody can trace, before landing in a basket with a price tag and not much else.

We wanted something different — clothing made from materials grown and sourced close to home, finished by people we know, that says something true on the outside because it's true on the inside too.

No filler claims, no greenwashing. Just good materials, honest work, and sayings worth standing behind.

How It's Made

From Field to Garment

Grown

Fibre grown locally and sustainably, season by season.

Spun

Raw fibre spun into yarn close to where it's grown.

Dyed

Coloured in small batches, with care for what runs downstream.

Sewn

Cut and finished by hand, piece by piece.
