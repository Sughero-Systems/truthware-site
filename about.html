About

The language came first.

The materials had to be honest enough to carry it.

TruthWare started as a question, not a product line: what would it look like for clothing to say something true, on purpose, to the people who already understand it?

The phrases came first. The garments are how they reach you.

None of that works if the materials underneath are dishonest about where they came from. So the fibre is grown and sourced close to home, and finished by people we know.

What that means in practice
Locally Grown Fibre and dye sourced from growers and makers within reach.
Hand Finished Every piece passes through real hands before it reaches yours.
Built to Last Made to stay in rotation for years, not a single season.
How It's Made

From Field to Garment

Grown

Fibre grown locally and sustainably, season by season.

Spun

Raw fibre spun into yarn close to where it's grown.

Dyed

Coloured in small batches, with care for what runs downstream.

Sewn

Cut and finished by hand, piece by piece.
